3. ProBono Clinics

The LAB is part of the Ministry of Law and provides legal aid in a wide range of civil matters.

These civil matters include:

  • Divorce
  • Variation or enforcement of Court orders on ancillary matters in divorce (e.g. maintenance of ex-wife and children, division of assets and properties)
  • Adoption
  • Custody of children
  • Monetary claim
  • Claim for compensation in injury or medical negligence cases
  • Estate matters, including the application for a Grant of Letters of Administration or Probate (documents authorising the personal representative(s) of a deceased to deal with and distribute the deceased’s assets)

The PBSO’s Project Law Help programme matches eligible community-serving organisations with law firms that are willing to provide free non-litigation commercial legal services.
